About Us

Ship With Tosin is not just another logistics company - we're your fashion-forward, food-loving shipping revolutionaries. Founded in 2015 by Tosin Adekunle (a former fashion designer turned logistics guru), we specialize in getting your precious cargo where it needs to go with care and attention.

Our journey began when Tosin struggled to ship her handmade African print dresses to international clients without them arriving wrinkled or delayed. Frustrated with existing options, she built a company that understands the unique needs of fashion entrepreneurs and food artisans.

Today, we move over 50,000 fashion items monthly for designers across 3 continents. Our temperature-controlled food division handles everything from artisanal cheeses to premium Nigerian spices. We've even developed special packaging solutions that keep garments wrinkle-free and food fresh during transit.
